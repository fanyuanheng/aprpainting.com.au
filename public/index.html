About APR

Why choose our team?

APR Painting Pty Ltd is an established professional painting group consisting of carefully selected, highly trained, qualified, and accredited Master Painters©, who take great pride in our impeccable work, excellent communication and customer service.

In divisions such as education, health, property and facilities maintenance, government, hospitality, industrial and commercial buildings; APR Painting Pty Ltd are the experts.

Using only the highest premium quality, APAS approved and environmentally aware paints, APR Painting Pty Ltd can tailor a specification to meet every building, and decorating need.

A full “low VOC” (green standards) specification is easily attained.

Over the years in business, we have painted hundreds of homes, units, factories, commercial office spaces and much more. There is no painting job in Melbourne too big that we can’t handle.
